About Me

My name is Chris Ridenour, and I am a recent Purdue University - West Lafayette graduate, having earned a bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering and a minor in Political Science. Currently I am serving in a software engineering role.

Previously, I spent two summers as an automation engineering intern at Cleveland-Cliffs in East Chicago, Indiana, working on projects involving PLCs, VB.net lab monitoring systems, and neural network data modeling. I've also served as a member on the executive board for several Purdue University student organizations, including the Purdue SIGBots VEX U robotics team and the Association for Computing Machinery.

Feel free to check out my website and my LinkedIn.
